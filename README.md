## General Hubs

- **[🆓 LibreTexts Medicine](https://med.libretexts.org/)** - Open-source medical learning guides and textbooks
- **[🆓 MedEdPORTAL](https://www.mededportal.org/)** - Free teaching materials including lecture notes, clinical cases, and quizzes from medical schools
- **[🆓 OpenMD Student Directory](https://openmd.com/directory/student)** - Directory of interactive learning tools and free medical textbooks
- **[🆓 r/MedicalSchool Wiki](https://www.reddit.com/r/medicalschool/wiki/index)** - Community-curated index of med school resources
- **[💲 Medscape](https://www.medscape.com/)** - Medical news, drug references, and clinical guidelines
- **[💲 Osmosis](https://www.osmosis.org/)** - Health education platform with video content for students and physicians

## Textbooks and Books

- **[🆓 Afratafreeh](https://afratafreeh.com/category/brands/)** - Medical Books &  Video Courses
- **[🆓 NIH / NCBI Bookshelf](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/)** - Full-text searchable medical textbooks hosted by the NIH
- **[🆓 NCBI StatPearls](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK430685/)** - Comprehensive educational articles with practice questions
- **[🆓 OpenStax](https://openstax.org/)** - Free peer-reviewed textbooks covering biology, anatomy, pharmacology, and more
- **[🆓 WHO Publications](https://www.who.int/publications)** - Downloadable clinical guidelines and manuals from the World Health Organization
- **[🆓 MSF Clinical Guidelines](https://medicalguidelines.msf.org/)** - Diagnosis and treatment manual covering respiratory, surgical, GI, and more
- **[🆓 infobooks.org - Medical PDFs](https://infobooks.org/free-pdf-books/medical/)** - 100+ free medical books from OpenStax, WHO, and Doctors Without Borders
- **[🆓 Free Books 4 Doctors](https://www.freebooks4doctors.com/)** - Directory of free medical books in PDF format from around the web
- **[🆓 Open Library](https://openlibrary.org/)** - 1.7M+ books available to borrow or read online, including medical titles
- **[🆓 DOAB](https://www.doabooks.org/)** - 1,500+ peer-reviewed open access academic books
- **[🆓 Wellcome Collection](https://wellcomecollection.org/)** - Digitized manuscripts, medical videos, and health-related art
- **[🆓 Open Michigan - Health Sciences](https://open.umich.edu/)** - Textbooks, courses, audio-video content, and datasets from University of Michigan
- **[🆓 FreemedicalBooks.com](https://freemedicalbooks.com/)** - Index of hundreds of free medical textbooks in PDF form

## Video Lectures

- **[🆓 Khan Academy - Medicine](https://www.khanacademy.org/science/health-and-medicine)** - Physiology and pathology videos, good for building foundational knowledge
- **[🆓 Ninja Nerd](https://www.youtube.com/@NinjaNerdOfficial)** - Whiteboard-style lectures on physiology, pathology, and pharmacology
- **[🆓 Osmosis YouTube](https://www.youtube.com/c/osmosis)** - Animated medical videos covering a wide range of topics
- **[🆓 Armando Hasudungan](https://www.youtube.com/@armandohasudungan)** - Illustrated biology and medical science explanations
- **[🆓 eLearning for Healthcare (e-LfH)](https://www.e-lfh.org.uk/)** - Interactive online learning for healthcare professionals
- **[💰 Lecturio](https://www.lecturio.com/)** - Full medical curriculum video courses
- **[💰 Boards and Beyond](https://www.boardsbeyond.com/)** - High-yield USMLE Step 1 and Step 2 video lectures

## Anatomy and Histology

- **[🆓 Get Body Smart](https://www.getbodysmart.com/)** - Interactive anatomy and physiology tutorials
- **[🆓 University of Michigan Anatomy](https://sites.google.com/a/umich.edu/bluelink/)** - High-yield cadaveric dissection photos and anatomy guides
- **[🆓 Inner Body](https://www.innerbody.com/)** - 2D and 3D anatomy atlas
- **[🆓 NIH 3D Print Exchange](https://3d.nih.gov/)** - Biomedical science 3D models from the NIH
- **[💲 Zygote Body](https://www.zygotebody.com/)** - Interactive 3D human anatomical models
- **[💲 AnatomyLearning](https://anatomylearning.com/)** - 3D anatomy learning platform
- **[💲 BioDigital Human](https://www.biodigital.com/)** - 3D anatomy and disease visualization tool
- **[💲 Kenhub](https://www.kenhub.com/)** - Anatomy illustrations, quizzes, and articles

## Radiology and Imaging

- **[🆓 Radiology Assistant](https://radiologyassistant.nl/)** - Educational radiology guides by the Radiological Society of the Netherlands
- **[🆓 Radiology Education](https://www.radiologyeducation.com/)** - Radiology educational references
- **[🆓 Sectional Anatomy](https://www.sectional-anatomy.org/)** - Cross-sectional MRI and CT scans for learning
- **[💲 Radiopaedia](https://radiopaedia.org/)** - Large open-edit radiology resource with thousands of real cases and expert reports

## Pharmacology

- **[🆓 Tulane University Pharmacology](https://tmedweb.tulane.edu/pharmwiki/doku.php/start)** - Pharmacology notes and quizzes
- **[🆓 OpenStax Pharmacology](https://openstax.org/details/books/pharmacology)** - Peer-reviewed pharmacology textbook covering all major drug classes
- **[🆓 MSF Essential Medicines](https://medicalguidelines.msf.org/en/viewport/EssDr/english/essential-drugs-16682376.html)** - Practical drug guide covering oral drugs, injectables, infusion fluids, and vaccines
- **[🆓 Antibiotics Summary](https://www.bugsanddrugs.org/)** - Quick-reference charts for antibiotic spectrums and coverage
- **[💰 Sketchy Medical](https://www.sketchy.com/)** - Visual mnemonic platform for pharmacology and microbiology

## Pathology and Physiology

- **[🆓 Pathology Outlines](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/)** - Free continuously updated reference covering surgical and clinical pathology
- **[🆓 University of Utah Pathology](https://webpath.med.utah.edu/)** - Large pathology image database with learning guides
- **[🆓 The Calgary Guide](https://calgaryguide.ucalgary.ca/)** - Disease pathophysiology and clinical manifestation flowcharts
- **[💲 Physeo](https://physeo.com/)** - Illustrated physiology articles and quizzes linked to clinical scenarios
- **[💰 Pathoma](https://www.pathoma.com/)** - Pathology video course and textbook widely used for USMLE Step 1

## Clinical Guides and References

- **[🆓 Geeky Medics](https://geekymedics.com/)** - Clinical skills guides, OSCE revision, and examination walkthroughs
- **[🆓 MSD Manuals - Professional](https://www.msdmanuals.com/professional)** - Comprehensive trusted medical reference for clinicians
- **[🆓 UC San Diego Clinical Medicine Guide](https://meded.ucsd.edu/clinicalmed/)** - Full clinical medicine guide from UCSD
- **[🆓 Easy Auscultation](https://www.easyauscultation.com/)** - Heart and lung sound audio tutorials
- **[🆓 Glowm.com](https://www.glowm.com/)** - 400+ full-text chapters on women's health with surgical videos and diagnostic atlases
- **[💲 Epocrates](https://www.epocrates.com/)** - Drug prescribing, interactions, and clinical guideline references
- **[💲 Cochrane Library](https://www.cochranelibrary.com/)** - Systematic reviews of healthcare interventions
- **[💰 UpToDate](https://www.uptodate.com/)** - Evidence-based clinical decision support used by practicing physicians

## Emergency Medicine

- **[🆓 EMCrit](https://emcrit.org/)** - Emergency medicine and critical care blog, podcast, and guides
- **[🆓 Life in the Fast Lane (LITFL)](https://litfl.com/)** - Free emergency medicine education, cases, and CME resources
- **[🆓 Emergency Medicine Cases](https://emergencymedicinecases.com/)** - Free EM podcast and case index
- **[🆓 DeployedMedicine](https://deployedmedicine.com/)** - Combat and field medicine guides, manuals, and videos

## Calculators and Tools

- **[🆓 MDCalc](https://www.mdcalc.com/)** - Leading medical calculator for clinical decision rules and scores
- **[🆓 Anki](https://apps.ankiweb.net/)** - Spaced repetition flashcard app, highly effective for long-term memorization
- **[🆓 Zotero](https://www.zotero.org/)** - Free reference manager for organising medical articles and papers
- **[🆓 Excalidraw](https://excalidraw.com/)** - Free digital whiteboard for concept mapping and diagrams
- **[🆓 Notion](https://www.notion.so/)** - Organize notes, schedules, and study plans

## Question Banks and Flashcards

- **[🆓 r/medicalschoolanki](https://www.reddit.com/r/medicalschoolanki/)** - Community for pre-made medical Anki decks including AnKing and Zanki
- **[🆓 OpenMD Question Bank](https://openmd.com/)** - Free 1,000+ question bank with peer-reviewed concept articles
- **[💲 Amboss](https://www.amboss.com/)** - Medical knowledge library and question bank for students and professionals
- **[💲 Brainscape - Medical](https://www.brainscape.com/subjects/medicine)** - Flashcard platform with pre-made medical sets
- **[💰 Passmedicine](https://www.passmedicine.com/)** - Question bank for UK medical exams including UCAT, PLAB, and MRCP
- **[💰 UWorld](https://www.uworld.com/)** - Industry-standard question bank for USMLE Step 1, 2, and 3

## Journals and Articles

- **[🆓 PubMed / PubMed Central (PMC)](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pmc/)** - Free full-text archive of biomedical and life sciences journal literature at the NIH
- **[🆓 Google Scholar](https://scholar.google.com/)** - Search engine for research articles, reviews, and clinical studies
- **[🆓 DOAJ - Directory of Open Access Journals](https://doaj.org/)** - Index of free full-text peer-reviewed medical journals
- **[🆓 MedEdPORTAL Journal](https://www.mededportal.org/)** - Open-access journal of health education teaching resources by AAMC
- **[💲 BMJ](https://www.bmj.com/)** - Leading general medical journal with some open-access content
- **[💲 The Lancet](https://www.thelancet.com/)** - High-impact medical journal with some free content
- **[💲 NEJM](https://www.nejm.org/)** - Premier medical journal with limited free access

## USMLE Prep

- **[🆓 USMLE.org](https://www.usmle.org/)** - Official site for the three-step U.S. medical licensing examination
- **[🆓 AnKing Anki Deck](https://www.ankingmed.com/)** - Pre-made comprehensive Anki deck built specifically for USMLE
- **[💲 Amboss](https://www.amboss.com/)** - Q-bank and knowledge library for USMLE prep
- **[💰 UWorld](https://www.uworld.com/)** - The most widely used USMLE question bank
- **[💰 Pathoma](https://www.pathoma.com/)** - Go-to pathology resource for Step 1
- **[💰 Boards and Beyond](https://www.boardsbeyond.com/)** - High-yield video lectures for Step 1 and Step 2
- **[💰 First Aid for USMLE Step 1](https://www.firstaidteam.com/)** - The standard USMLE Step 1 prep book
- **[💰 Sketchy Medical](https://www.sketchy.com/)** - Visual mnemonic stories for microbiology and pharmacology
- **[💰 Picmonic](https://www.picmonic.com/)** - Illustrated mnemonic learning for pharmacology and pathology
